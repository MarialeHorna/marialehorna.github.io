**Role:** Green Investment Analyst, GPD Partners
**Geography:** Peru · Ecuador
**Period:** 2025
**Portfolio size:** USD 14M across 12 active investments

## Context

GPD Partners invests in sustainable agriculture, water management, and climate-resilient infrastructure in Andean markets — sectors where impact and financial discipline have to be reconciled deal by deal. The portfolio combines SME lending, equity into cooperatives, and structured instruments for climate-resilient value chains.

## What I did

I managed a USD 14M portfolio across 12 active investments, balancing financial-performance targets with environmental and social objectives. I originated and structured 12 debt and equity transactions for SMEs and agricultural cooperatives, producing the financial models and risk assessments that informed USD 6M in investment decisions. I led ESG due diligence for 8 potential investees, evaluating biodiversity impacts, climate-adaptation measures, and supply-chain vulnerabilities in high-risk geographies. I built KPI and impact-reporting frameworks aligned with IFC Performance Standards, IRIS+, and the SDGs, and provided technical assistance to portfolio companies on financial planning and climate-smart agricultural practices.

## Outcome

A portfolio that delivered on both financial and impact dimensions across Peru and Ecuador, with reporting infrastructure that scales — and a body of underwriting know-how at the intersection of impact investing, agricultural value chains, and climate adaptation.
