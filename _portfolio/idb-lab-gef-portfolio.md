**Role:** Climate Investment Associate, IDB Lab
**Geography:** Seven Latin American countries
**Period:** 2025 – Present
**Status:** Active

## Context

IDB Lab — the innovation laboratory of the Inter-American Development Bank Group — backs ventures and instruments that address climate, inclusion, and digital-economy challenges across Latin America and the Caribbean. The climate portfolio combines equity, debt, blended finance, and technical assistance, and is partly co-financed by the Global Environment Facility (GEF).

## What I do

I source and assess equity, debt, and blended-finance opportunities for climate and energy investments across seven Latin American countries — running due diligence, financial modeling, market analyses, and investment memoranda. I structure transactions in coordination with governments, accelerators, and private investors, designing blended-finance mechanisms that de-risk capital into climate-resilient agriculture, the circular economy, and clean technology. I help manage and monitor the GEF co-financed portfolio, conducting risk assessments and supporting technical assistance to strengthen governance and financial performance. I also contribute to the broader climate-investment strategy of the Lab, supporting pipeline development and alignment across regional operations.

## Outcome

An active portfolio across Latin American climate and energy investments, structured to combine catalytic public capital with private and impact-aligned investors — and a working stake in shaping how IDB Lab's climate strategy lands on the ground.
