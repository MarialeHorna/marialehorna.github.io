## Climate finance and development economist working at the intersection of capital, policy, and emerging markets.

I am an economist building a career across **climate finance, development, and emerging-markets policy**. My work moves between structuring climate investments and designing the policy frameworks that channel capital where it is most needed.

I am currently pursuing a **MSc in Climate Finance at Columbia Climate School** as a [Stanley Park Scholar](#) (full-tuition merit award, three recipients per cohort), while serving as **Climate Investment Associate at [IDB Lab](https://bidlab.org/en)**, sourcing and structuring equity, debt, and blended-finance transactions across seven Latin American countries.

Before Columbia, I co-designed **Peru's Green Taxonomy** with the Ministry of Economy and the country's financial regulators at GIZ, structured a national green-finance pipeline expected to mobilize **USD 100M+**, and managed a **USD 14M sustainable agriculture and water portfolio** at GPD Partners. My research on sustainability certifications and agro-export competitiveness was published in the *Journal of Business* (Universidad del Pacífico, 2025).

I work in English, Spanish, German, and Portuguese, and write between New York and Lima.
