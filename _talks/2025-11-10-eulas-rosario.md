Speaker at the **2025 International Edition of EULAS** (EU–Latin America Studies), organized by the **Jean Monnet Network** and hosted at **Universidad Nacional de Rosario** (Argentina), 10–12 November 2025.

I presented the research paper **"Effect of Voluntary Certifications on Non-Traditional Agro-Exports in Peru"**, which examines — using Propensity Score Matching combined with a Difference-in-Differences estimator — whether holding voluntary private certifications (with a focus on GlobalGAP) improves the export value of Peruvian agroexporting firms, and how these effects vary by firm size.

The conference convened researchers and policy practitioners from European and Latin American institutions working at the intersection of trade, sustainable development, and EU–LAC cooperation.
