Presenter at the **1° Congreso Internacional IKSAD–UPC: "Economía sostenible en entornos cambiantes"**, hosted in Lima, Peru on **4–5 June 2025**.

I presented the paper **"Efecto de las Certificaciones Voluntarias sobre las agroexportaciones no tradicionales en el Perú"** (coauthored with Bertha Mendoza, Universidad del Pacífico) on **4 June 2025**, in **Mesa 3 — Exportaciones en el Perú** (Aula E57), moderated by Karina Bustamante and Wendy Montano (UPC).

The paper examines whether voluntary private certifications — particularly GlobalGAP — improve the export value of Peruvian agroexporting firms, using Propensity Score Matching combined with a Difference-in-Differences estimator. Results show a positive effect at the firm level, with the exception of micro-enterprises, suggesting that scale and absorptive capacity mediate the returns to voluntary sustainability standards.

The congress, organized jointly by **IKSAD Institute** and **Universidad Peruana de Ciencias Aplicadas (UPC)**, brought together researchers from Latin America and beyond working on sustainable economics, trade, and policy in changing global environments.
